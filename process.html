How we work

Six steps to a fully operational HR function.
No migration.

No lengthy implementation. No new software to learn. We work inside your existing tools and systems — and you have a fully operational HR function within two weeks.

1
Week 1, Day 1

Discovery Call

A focused 45-minute conversation about your current state — team size, tools, pain points, open roles, and goals. We ask the 20 questions that reveal what's working and what's broken. No pitch deck. No templates. Just a genuine assessment of your people operations.

Deliverable: Written summary of current state and top 5 priority gaps · 24 hours
2
Week 1, Day 2–3

HR Systems Audit

We review your existing HR stack — whatever tools you use, however documented your processes are. We identify gaps, compliance risks, and quick wins. You get a clear written audit report with a priority-ordered remediation plan within 48 hours. This alone is worth the engagement.

Deliverable: Written compliance audit report with prioritised risk register · 48 hours
3
Week 1–2

Process & Policy Design

We build the HR infrastructure your company actually needs — not a generic template. Employee handbook tailored to your jurisdiction and culture. Employment contract library. Core policy suite (leave, remote work, expenses, code of conduct, grievance). Every document built for your specific situation.

Deliverable: Complete policy and documentation library · Branded PDF · Ready to deploy
4
Week 2

System Integration

We configure everything inside your existing tools — HR platform, Notion, Google Workspace, Slack, or whatever your team already uses. No migration projects. No learning curve. Your team continues in familiar systems; they just work significantly better. We also set up reporting dashboards and compliance tracking.

Deliverable: Live HR infrastructure in your existing tools · Reporting dashboards configured
5
End of Week 2

Go Live

Your HR function is operational. We brief your leadership team on the new processes, provide manager guides, and activate the Slack or WhatsApp support channel. From this point, you have a fully functional HR department — without hiring one. The first monthly dashboard arrives 30 days later.

Deliverable: Go-live briefing · Manager guides · Support channel activated · Month 1 plan
6
Month 1 onwards

Ongoing Operations

We run your HR function on retainer — handling hiring requests, onboarding coordination, compliance questions, documentation updates, and strategic people ops. Monthly dashboard delivered automatically. Quarterly strategic review included. Direct Slack or WhatsApp access for any question that can't wait for a meeting.

Ongoing: Monthly people dashboard · Hiring support · Compliance monitoring · Slack/WhatsApp access
