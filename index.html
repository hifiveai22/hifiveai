Vision

Common questions.

Is there a free trial? ↓
Yes. All plans come with a 7-day free trial with full access to the AI interview engine and scoring platform. No credit card required to start.
What currencies do you accept? ↓
We accept INR, USD, SGD, and GBP. Enterprise contracts can be invoiced in any major currency with NET-30 terms.
Can I upgrade or downgrade mid-cycle? ↓
Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time — the difference is prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
How does EOR pricing work? ↓
EOR is priced per employee per month and varies by country. Typical rates range from $180–$480/employee/month including payroll, benefits, and compliance. Speak to our team for a quote specific to your target countries.
Do you charge per hire or per role? ↓
No per-hire or success fees on platform plans. You pay your monthly plan fee and get access to all features. Executive Search engagements have a separate retainer + success fee structure — speak to our team.
